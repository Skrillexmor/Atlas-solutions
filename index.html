FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for internships at Atlas SoftWeb?
Any student pursuing B.Tech, BCA, MCA, or diploma courses in IT/CS can apply. Freshers and graduates looking to upskill are also welcome.
Is the internship online or offline?
We offer both online (remote) and offline (in-person) internship modes. You can choose based on your preference and location.
What is the duration of the internship programs?
Our internship programs typically run for 1 to 3 months, depending on the technology domain and your learning pace.
Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?
Yes, every intern who successfully completes the program receives an official industry-recognized internship completion certificate from Atlas SoftWeb Pvt Ltd.
Do you help with final year projects?
Absolutely. We provide complete guidance and development support for final year projects across all our domains — DevOps, Laravel, Android, Python, and Full Stack.
How can a business request a custom project?
Visit our Contact page and fill out the project request form. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your requirements.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes. We provide resume building, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock interview sessions, and referrals for placement opportunities.
