Who We Are

Empowering Tech Talent Since Day One

Atlas SoftWeb Pvt Ltd is a technology company based in Surat, Gujarat, providing IT services, project development, internship programs, and corporate training to students, freshers, colleges, startups, and businesses across India.

We bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by offering real-world project experience under the guidance of seasoned professionals. Our programs are designed to make learners job-ready from day one.
